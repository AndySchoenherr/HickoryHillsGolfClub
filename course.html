Hickory Hills Golf Club

The par-35, 9-hole golf course at Hickory Hills Golf Course in Brownstown, Indiana measures over 3100 yards from the back tees. It was designed by Harold England and opened in 1973. The manageable length of our golf course is a challange of accuracy and skill for all levels.
